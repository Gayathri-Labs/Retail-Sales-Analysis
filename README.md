# Retail Sales Analysis #

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes a retail sales dataset using PostgreSQL and Microsoft Excel to understand business performance, customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends.

The project focuses on solving real business questions with SQL queries and presenting the insights through an interactive Excel dashboard.

---

##  Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing products, customers, and regions
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Track monthly sales trends
- Support business decisions with data-driven insights

---

##  Tools Used

- PostgreSQL
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers

---

##  Dataset

The dataset contains retail sales information, including:

- Order Details
- Customer Information
- Product Line
- Country & City
- Sales
- Quantity Ordered
- Deal Size
- Order Status
- Order Date

---

##  Business Questions

1. What is the total sales and total number of orders?
2. Which product line has the highest sales, and which has the lowest?
3. Which country has the highest total sales?
4. Which city has the highest total sales?
5. Who are the top 10 customers based on total sales?
6. Which customer has not placed an order for the longest time?
7. Which product line has the highest order quantity, and which has the highest sales?
8. Which product line has the highest price, and which has the lowest price?
9. How many orders are there in each order status?
10. Which deal size has the highest total sales?
11. How do sales change month by month?
12. Which month has the highest total sales?
13. Which product line has the highest sales in each country?
14. Which product line has the highest sales and which has the lowest sales based on quantity ordered?
15. Which customers, countries, product lines, and deal sizes generate the highest sales?

---

##  Dashboard

The Excel dashboard includes:

- Total Sales KPI
- Total Orders KPI
- Total Customers KPI
- Total Countries KPI
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Product Line
- Sales by Country
- Top 10 Customers
- Order Status Distribution
- Deal Size Contribution
- Interactive Slicers

---

##  Key Insights

- Overall sales and order performance were analyzed.
- Top-performing products, customers, countries, and cities were identified.
- Monthly sales trends helped highlight seasonal performance.
- Deal size analysis showed which segment generated the highest revenue.
- Customer and product insights can support better business decisions.

---

##  Outcome

This project demonstrates practical SQL querying, Excel dashboard creation, and business analysis skills by answering real-world business questions and presenting the results in a clear and interactive dashboard.
